by mjsullivan2021@protonmail.com | Available June 5, 2025 |Courses | 0 comments

Overview

An Advanced Prompt Engineering Course for Business Leaders: Deepen AI capabilities for strategic business growth. This self-paced program equips non-technical leaders with frameworks and prompt templates to direct AI toward high-impact outcomes across decision-making, operations, and competitive strategy.

Strategic Value

This course provides systematic approaches to integrate AI beyond experimentation. You’ll gain skills to elevate strategic insights, optimize workflows, strengthen competitive positioning, and drive measurable growth through advanced prompt engineering.

Course Structure

An 8-module self-paced program adaptable to a 6-week intensive or 12-week gradual pace. Each module builds on the previous, delivering advanced frameworks and prompt templates as enduring business assets.

Module 1: AI Foundations & Tool Setup

Establish fundamental AI understanding and configure a cost-effective professional toolkit tailored for business leaders.

Module 2: The SIMPLE Framework

Learn our six-step framework guiding AI toward consistent, professional-quality outputs for diverse business challenges.

Module 3: Zero-Shot Prompting Mastery

Master prompts that yield immediate high-quality insights without examples, for quick strategic recommendations.

Module 4: Few-Shot Prompting & Examples

Use strategic examples to guide AI behavior, improving accuracy for communications, marketing content, and complex planning.

Module 5: Workflow Integration & Automation

Transform individual AI tasks into integrated workflows that enhance operations and efficiency.

Module 6: Team Implementation

Scale AI capabilities across teams, foster collaborative AI workflows, and manage knowledge transfer.

Module 7: Advanced Applications

Explore sophisticated AI uses: strategic analysis, competitive intelligence, and executive-level problem-solving.

Module 8: Optimization & Future Planning

Develop frameworks to measure AI’s impact, optimize prompt systems, and plan long-term AI capability growth.
